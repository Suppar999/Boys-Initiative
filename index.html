Learning Modules

Gender, Masculinity & Equality

This module explores the concepts of gender, masculinity, and equality, aiming to dismantle harmful stereotypes and promote inclusive attitudes. Participants learn about the societal constructs of gender roles, the impact of toxic masculinity, and the importance of advocating for gender equality. Through interactive discussions and real-world examples, youth are encouraged to challenge biases, foster respect, and build equitable communities. Key topics include understanding gender stereotypes, promoting healthy masculinity, and recognizing the value of equal opportunities for all.

Learn more

Safe vs. Unsafe Touch

The Safe vs. Unsafe Touch module equips young people with the knowledge to identify appropriate and inappropriate physical interactions. It emphasizes the importance of personal boundaries and empowers youth to assertively say "no" to uncomfortable situations. The session includes practical guidance on recognizing unsafe touch, understanding bodily autonomy, and knowing how to report incidents to trusted adults or authorities. Through role-playing and discussions, participants gain confidence in protecting themselves and supporting others in maintaining personal safety.

Learn more

Child Marriage, Labor & Trafficking

This module addresses critical issues affecting youth, including child marriage, child labor, and human trafficking. It educates participants about their rights under Indian laws, such as the POCSO Act and Juvenile Justice Act, and highlights warning signs of exploitation. The session empowers youth to recognize and report suspicious activities, ensuring they know how to seek help. Through case studies and group activities, participants learn about the consequences of these issues and how to advocate for child protection in their communities.

Learn more

Relationships, Consent & Boundaries

The Relationships, Consent & Boundaries module focuses on fostering healthy interpersonal relationships among youth. It covers the principles of mutual respect, effective communication, and the importance of consent in all interactions. Participants learn to set and respect personal boundaries, recognize signs of unhealthy relationships, and practice assertive communication. Through interactive scenarios and group discussions, this session helps youth build skills to navigate friendships, romantic relationships, and family dynamics with confidence and respect.

Learn more

Mental Health, Bullying & Peer Pressure

This module provides a safe space to discuss mental health, bullying, and peer pressure, equipping youth with tools to navigate these challenges. It covers strategies for managing stress, recognizing signs of mental health struggles, and seeking help when needed. The session also addresses how to respond to bullying, both as a victim and a bystander, and how to resist negative peer pressure. Through workshops and peer-sharing activities, participants learn coping mechanisms and build resilience to maintain their mental well-being.

Learn more

Puberty, Masturbation & Body Positivity

The Puberty, Masturbation & Body Positivity module offers a supportive environment to discuss physical and emotional changes during adolescence. It provides accurate information about puberty, addresses myths and stigmas around masturbation, and promotes body positivity. Participants learn to embrace their changing bodies, develop a healthy body image, and practice self-acceptance. Through open discussions and educational content, this session helps youth navigate adolescence with confidence and a positive self-image, free from societal pressures.

Meet ANI Chatbot

"Namaskar! I'm Ani.
